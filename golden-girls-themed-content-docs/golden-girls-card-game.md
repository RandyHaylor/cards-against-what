# Thank You for Being a Friend: The Unofficial Golden Girls Card Game
=====================================================================

*A party game for fabulous people.*
*Inspired by Cards Against Humanity. Themed around The Golden Girls.*
*Innuendo-forward. Cheesecake included.*

---

## HOW TO PLAY
Same as Cards Against Humanity. One player draws a black (question) card
and reads it aloud. All other players choose their funniest white (answer)
card to fill the blank. The reader picks their favorite. That player
gets the point. Rotate readers. Eat cheesecake.
